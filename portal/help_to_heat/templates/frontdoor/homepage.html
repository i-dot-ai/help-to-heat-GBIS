Use this service to:

  • check if your household and property may be able to qualify for ECO4 or the GBIS
  • create a referral to an energy supplier

Who can use this service

  • homeowners
  • tenants who rent from a private landlord or estate agent
  • tenants who live in social housing

Tenants will need permissions from their landlords to use this service.

Energy-saving measures you could qualify for includes

  • cavity or solid wall insulation
  • external wall insulation
  • loft insulation
  • flat or pitched roof insulation
  • park home insulation

What happens once you create a referral:

  • The energy supplier will contact you within 10 working days
  • They will assess the property and determine the most energy-efficient measures that you qualify for
  • They will install the agreed measures at a lower cost to you

Before you start:

You will be asked for information about:

  • your household income
  • any benefits you, or anyone in your household receives
  • the council tax band of the property
  • details of the walls and insulation in the property

If you are unsure about your EPC rating or council tax band, this service can help you find that information.

You will be asked to give permission to pass your details onto a supplier that you choose before you are referred. If you do not want to supply your information or go forward with the application, you can close the tool at any time before confirming and submitting.
